<p><p>The RO water purifier market is categorized into Point of Use Reverse Osmosis (POU RO) and Point of Entry Reverse Osmosis (POE RO) systems. POU RO water purifiers are designed for individual consumption, typically installed at specific outlets like kitchen taps, ensuring treated water is readily available. In contrast, POE RO systems filter water at the main water supply entry point of a home or facility, providing purified water to all outlets, offering comprehensive protection for overall water quality and usage.</p></p>
