<p><p>The steel pipe coatings market encompasses various types designed to enhance corrosion resistance and durability. Epoxy coatings provide a strong barrier against chemicals and moisture. Fusion Bond Epoxy (FBE) offers superior adhesion and is applied as a powder. Cement-mortar coatings offer additional protection through a thickened cement layer. Polyurethane coatings are known for their flexibility and UV resistance. Bituminous asphalt coatings provide a weather-resistant layer, while "Others" include innovative solutions like polyethylene and rubberized coatings, catering to diverse application needs.</p></p>
