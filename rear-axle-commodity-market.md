<p><p>The rear axle commodity market comprises three main types: lift axles, dead axles, and drive axles. Lift axles are designed to enhance load capacity by being engaged when needed, optimizing vehicle weight distribution. Dead axles do not receive power and serve primarily for support, often found in trailers. Drive axles, on the other hand, provide power to the wheels for traction, playing a crucial role in the vehicle's movement. Each type serves specific purposes in transportation and logistics.</p></p>
