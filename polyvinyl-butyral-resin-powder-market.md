<p><p>Polyvinyl Butyral (PVB) resin powder is categorized into three main types based on molecular weight: higher, medium, and lower molecular weight grades. Higher molecular weight grade PVB offers enhanced mechanical properties and chemical resistance, making it suitable for applications like automotive clear laminates. Medium molecular weight grade balances performance and processability, frequently used in adhesives and sealants. Lower molecular weight grade PVB provides good flexibility and adhesion, often finding use in coatings and films. Each grade serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
