<p><p>The femoral venous catheter market includes unipolar and bipolar femoral arterial cannulae, which are vital for cardiovascular procedures. Unipolar femoral arterial cannulae feature a single lumen, providing straightforward blood flow management, while bipolar femoral arterial cannulae have dual lumens, allowing for simultaneous blood drainage and infusion. Both types are employed in settings such as surgeries and critical care, enhancing patient outcomes by facilitating effective hemodynamic monitoring and access during procedures requiring venous and arterial interventions.</p></p>
