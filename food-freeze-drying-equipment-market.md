<p><p>The food freeze-drying equipment market is divided into two main types: batch freeze-drying equipment and continuous freeze-drying equipment. Batch freeze-drying involves processing a single load at a time, allowing for flexibility and variety in product types, commonly used for smaller-scale operations. In contrast, continuous freeze-drying equipment processes materials in a continuous flow, enabling high-volume production and efficiency, suitable for large-scale manufacturing. Both types cater to different production needs in the food industry, offering varying capacities and operational dynamics.</p></p>
