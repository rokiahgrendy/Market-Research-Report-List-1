<p><p>The Controlled Atmosphere (CA) refrigerated container market is divided into two main types: active atmosphere and passive atmosphere. Active atmosphere containers utilize technology to regulate gas composition, such as oxygen and carbon dioxide, ensuring optimal conditions for preserving perishable goods. This method enhances shelf life and quality. Conversely, passive atmosphere containers rely on the natural properties of the materials to maintain an altered atmosphere without active intervention, making them a cost-effective option for certain applications.</p></p>
