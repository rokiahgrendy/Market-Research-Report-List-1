<p><p>The synthetic butadiene market encompasses various production methods, notably oxidative dehydrogenation and extractive distillation. Oxidative dehydrogenation involves converting hydrocarbons into butadiene using oxygen, enhancing efficiency and reducing waste. In contrast, extractive distillation separates butadiene from other byproducts through selective solvents, improving purity. Both techniques are essential for producing high-quality synthetic butadiene, which is crucial for the manufacture of synthetic rubber and other polymers, catering to growing demand across various industrial applications.</p></p>
