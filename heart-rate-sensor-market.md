<p><strong>The Heart Rate Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoplethysmography Sensors.</li><li>Electrocardiography Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heart rate sensor market is primarily categorized into photoplethysmography (PPG) and electrocardiography (ECG) sensors. PPG sensors utilize light to detect blood volume changes in the microvascular bed of tissue, enabling continuous heart rate monitoring, commonly found in wearable devices. In contrast, ECG sensors measure the electrical activity of the heart, providing more precise heart rate and rhythm information, often used in clinical settings. Both sensor types cater to diverse applications, ranging from fitness tracking to medical diagnostics.</p></p>
