<p><strong>The Cold Mix Asphalt Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emulsion-based Cold Mix Asphalt Additive</li><li>Solvent-based Cold Mix Asphalt Additive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cold Mix Asphalt Additives market is primarily divided into two types: emulsion-based and solvent-based additives. Emulsion-based cold mix asphalt additives utilize asphalt emulsions that blend well with aggregates, providing enhanced adhesion and workability. They are often favored for their environmental benefits and ease of application. In contrast, solvent-based cold mix asphalt additives consist of asphalt and solvents, which improve the viscosity and performance of asphalt mixes, making them suitable for various road maintenance and construction applications.</p></p>
