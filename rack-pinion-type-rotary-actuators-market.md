<p><p>Rack & pinion type rotary actuators are vital in converting rotary motion into linear motion. The market is generally divided into two types: single rack and double rack. Single rack actuators utilize a single gear mechanism, offering simplicity and suitability for moderate torque applications. In contrast, double rack actuators employ two gear sets, enhancing torque output and responsiveness, making them ideal for heavier loads and faster operations. Both types cater to various industrial applications, providing efficient motion control solutions.</p></p>
