<p><p>The Foldable Hinge market is categorized into two main types: Single Shaft and Multiple Shaft hinges. Single Shaft hinges consist of a single pivot point, allowing for simple folding mechanisms and compact designs, making them ideal for lightweight applications. In contrast, Multiple Shaft hinges feature several pivot points, enabling more complex movements and better support for heavier structures. These designs cater to diverse industries, including furniture, automotive, and electronics, each requiring specific functionality and durability in their folding applications.</p></p>
