<p><p>The wearable exoskeleton market is categorized into three main types: upper wearable, lower wearable, and body wearable. Upper wearable exoskeletons enhance arm and upper body strength, assisting users in tasks that require lifting or precision. Lower wearable exoskeletons focus on supporting the legs, helping with mobility, rehabilitation, and load-bearing activities. Body wearable exoskeletons integrate both upper and lower support, offering comprehensive assistance for a wide range of physical tasks, thus improving overall user efficiency and reducing strain.</p></p>
