<p><p>The photovoltaics microinverters market consists of two main types: grid-connected and grid-off microinverters. Grid-connected PV microinverters enable solar systems to feed excess energy back into the electrical grid, ensuring efficient energy use and financial benefits through net metering. In contrast, grid-off microinverters are designed for off-grid applications, allowing systems to operate independently, providing power in remote areas without grid access. Both types enhance energy conversion at the module level, improving overall system performance and reliability.</p></p>
