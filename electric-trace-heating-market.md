<p><p>Electric trace heating market encompasses various types: </p><p>1. **Self-Regulating** systems adjust their heat output based on the ambient temperature, ensuring optimal performance.</p><p>2. **Constant Wattage** provides consistent heat distribution, suitable for specific applications requiring steady temperatures.</p><p>3. **Mineral Insulated** cables offer high heat and mechanical protection, ideal for harsh environments.</p><p>4. **Series Resistance** involves resistive heating along the cable, maintaining warmth over a distance.</p><p>5. **Skin Tracing** targets heating the surface skin of pipes or equipment, facilitating effective temperature maintenance.</p></p>
