<p><p>The dried skimmed milk powder (SMP) market is categorized into three types based on the heat treatment during processing: Low Heat SMP, Medium Heat SMP, and High Heat SMP. Low Heat SMP is produced at lower temperatures, retaining more nutrients and is ideal for infant formula and dairy products. Medium Heat SMP has a balanced output suitable for various applications, while High Heat SMP, processed at higher temperatures, offers extended shelf life and is commonly used in bakery and confectionery products.</p></p>
