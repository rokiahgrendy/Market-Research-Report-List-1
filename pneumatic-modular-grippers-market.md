<p><p>The pneumatic modular grippers market primarily features two types: parallel grippers and angular grippers. Parallel grippers maintain a consistent orientation as they open and close, making them ideal for handling flat or symmetrical objects. In contrast, angular grippers pivot at an angle, allowing for the grasping of irregularly shaped items or those in tight spaces. Both types are integral in automation and robotics, offering versatility and efficiency in a range of industrial applications.</p></p>
