<p><p>The Penstock Plate market is categorized into two main types based on spindle design: non-rising and rising spindle. Non-rising spindle penstocks feature a mechanism where the valve's spindle remains stationary during operation, making them ideal for spaces with height restrictions. In contrast, rising spindle penstocks have a spindle that extends upwards when the valve opens, providing a visual indication of the valve position. Both types serve crucial roles in controlling water flow in various hydraulic systems.</p></p>
