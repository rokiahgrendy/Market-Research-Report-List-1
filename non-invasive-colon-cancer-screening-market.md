<p><strong>The Non Invasive Colon Cancer Screening Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fecal Immunochemical Test (FIT)</li><li>Guaiac-based Fecal Occult Blood Test (gFOBT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-Invasive Colon Cancer Screening Market primarily includes two key types: Fecal Immunochemical Test (FIT) and Guaiac-based Fecal Occult Blood Test (gFOBT). FIT detects specific human hemoglobin in stool samples, offering higher sensitivity and ease of use. In contrast, gFOBT identifies general blood presence in stool without specificity, requiring dietary restrictions before testing. Both methods provide non-invasive options for early detection of colorectal cancer, promoting higher screening participation rates and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
