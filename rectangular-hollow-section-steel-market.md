<p><p>The Rectangular Hollow Section (RHS) steel market comprises mainly two types: welded RHS and seamless RHS. Welded RHS is produced by welding flat steel plates into rectangular shapes, offering cost-effectiveness and versatility for various structural applications. Conversely, seamless RHS is crafted from solid steel billets, providing enhanced strength and uniformity, ideal for high-pressure and demanding structural environments. Each type serves distinct needs across construction, automotive, and manufacturing sectors, influencing their market dynamics and applications.</p></p>
