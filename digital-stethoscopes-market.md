<p><p>The digital stethoscope market primarily comprises two types: amplifying stethoscopes and digitizing stethoscopes. Amplifying stethoscopes enhance sound quality and volume, allowing healthcare professionals to better detect subtle heart and lung sounds. Digitizing stethoscopes convert acoustic sounds into digital signals, enabling storage, analysis, and sharing of data for telemedicine applications. Both types improve diagnostic accuracy and facilitate remote consultations, making them essential tools in modern healthcare settings where precision and accessibility are paramount.</p></p>
