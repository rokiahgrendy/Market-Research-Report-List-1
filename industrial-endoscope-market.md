<p><p>The industrial endoscope market comprises two primary types: fiberscopes and rigid borescopes. Fiberscopes utilize flexible fiber optic technology, allowing for inspection in tight or complex spaces, making them ideal for scenarios where versatility is needed. Rigid borescopes, on the other hand, feature a solid, straight construction, providing high-resolution imagery for inspections in well-defined access points. Both types are essential in various industries, including manufacturing, aerospace, and automotive, to enhance maintenance, troubleshooting, and quality control processes.</p></p>
