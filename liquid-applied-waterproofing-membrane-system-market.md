<p><p>The Liquid Applied Waterproofing Membrane System market primarily consists of two types: NCM (Non-Cementitious Membranes) and NCA (Cementitious Membranes). NCM refers to membranes made from various polymers, providing flexibility and resistance to environmental factors, making them ideal for demanding applications. NCA, on the other hand, is cement-based and offers excellent adhesion and durability, primarily used for surfaces exposed to moisture. Both types serve critical roles in protecting structures from water ingress and enhancing longevity.</p></p>
